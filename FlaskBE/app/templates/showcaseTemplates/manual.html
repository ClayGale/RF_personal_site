Personal Computer Building Manual

Written for CMNS 113 - Technical communications for information technology

Partner Project with Michael McClain

This manual is an elaborate 18 step guide to building a personal computer from parts. Each step is written as concisely as possible given the complexity of the task. Figures, tips, and warnings are presented throughout to provide relevant info without breaking the main guide flow.

I have sent this manual to multiple friends who were building a PC and they each used it with success. My belief is that the quality of this manual speaks for itself.
